Dr. Mikaela Stewart

Principal Investigator · Department of Biology, TCU

Dr. Stewart leads the lab's work on the structure and function of BRCA1 and its role in cancer risk, supported by the National Institutes of Health. She mentors students through every stage of real research — from purifying proteins to publishing — and is committed to making that science clear to people far outside the lab.
