Structural Biology · Texas Christian University

Every protein has a shape. That shape is a story.

In the Stewart Lab we read that story in BRCA1 — the tumor suppressor whose structure helps decide who is at risk for breast and ovarian cancer.

See the science Join the lab

Why it matters

Genetic testing can find inherited changes in the BRCA1 gene — but for thousands of those changes, no one yet knows whether they are dangerous or harmless. A patient can receive a result that, in effect, says we don't know. We work to change that answer.

Turning “unknown” into understood — one protein at a time.
